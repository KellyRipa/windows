Home

Welcome, and thank you for visiting Hyper Wipers Window Washing. Here at Hyper Wipers we've been brightening bay area homes since 2004. Whether you need your windows washed, rain gutters cleaned or solar panels cleaned, Hyper Wipers is the right company for the job. Our residential specialists will get the job done with the professionalism you expect and the friendliness you deserve.

One of the things that separate us from the other guys is that we are residential specialists. That means that not only will we do a wonderful job cleaning your windows, rain gutters or solar panels, but that we take all of the necessary measures to ensure that we have zero impact on your home. Shoe covers are always available whenever necessary or by request. Your furnishings and belongings will be treated with the utmost care and respect. If we need to move something we'll move it back. Great care is taken not to damage the landscaping around your home. Our ladders are only operated with non-marking polyurethane end caps to prevent damage to your walls. All walkways are swept clean of any debris that may have fallen while cleaning rain gutters. Our workers will not have any radios blaring and are sure to keep all conversations at a low level. Many of our customers that work from home say they really appreciate how quietly we work. We think you will too.

Please feel free to go over our website where we hope you will find the answers to any questions you may have. If you still have a question, would like an estimate, or to make an appointment please give us a call, email, or use our Contact Us page. Thank you, and we look forward to hearing from you.
