Services


Window Washing

Whether you need your windows washed inside and out or just the outsides only, Hyper Wipers is equipped to get the job done safely, efficiently, and consistently well. We wash all windows by hand, using professional window washing supplies. No dish soap or homemade concoctions here. Drop cloths are placed at the bottom of every window we wash while on the interior of your home. On the exterior, our workers have the experience necessary to get those hard to reach windows, safely.

Rain Gutter Cleaning

Our workers have the training, experience, and equipment to get to and clean those hard to reach rain gutters. When cleaning rain gutters, all debris will be scooped out by hand, bagged, and then disposed of in the proper receptacle. All walkways are swept clean of any debris that may have fallen while cleaning the rain gutters. All downspouts are checked to see that they are not clogged. If needed, clogged downspouts will be flushed out to ensure that they are flowing freely.

Solar Panel Cleaning

You know rain alone will not clean your car or your homes windows, so how would it get your solar panels clean? Both manufacturers and professional installers recommend cleaning your solar panels at least once a year to ensure that they function at their optimum level of performance. We can get those solar panels all washed up so that they continue to do what you bought them for, saving the environment AND saving you money.

Glass Restoration

Do you have windows or shower doors that won't come clean no matter what you try? Your glass may be stained due to oxidation, hard water minerals, swimming pool sweeps, sprinklers, etc. When this happens you have two options, replace the glass or glass restoration. Our specially designed tools and non-toxic, biodegradable stain remover can do wonders to polish out that stubborn build up, at a fraction of the cost of replacing the glass. This kind of build up will continue to get worse the longer you wait, so calling us sooner rather than later will yield much better results.
